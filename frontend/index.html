Enter your username
Step 1: Click on your planet and drag your mouse in the direction that you would like your planet to move in. This is your velocity.
Step 2: Click on the move button. You will find the statuses of the other players on the right hand side of the game. If a player has not yet moved, their status will show up as '...'
Step 3: Once everyone has clicked 'move' watch the bowls collide together.
Step 4: Repeat this process for the next 4 rounds unless your bowl has 'died', i.e. been yeeted outside of the playing field.
Step 5: Have fun & play with all your friends!
